üìù Custom Number Input

When you start the visualizer, you can:

  • Use Random Numbers: Press Enter or leave the input empty
  • Enter Custom Numbers: Type your own numbers

Input Format:

  • Separate numbers with spaces or commas
  • Maximum 25 numbers allowed
  • Numbers must be between 1 and 1000

Examples:

10 5 8 3 7 12 15 9 Using spaces
10,5,8,3,7,12,15,9 Using commas
100 50 25 75 90 60 30 Larger numbers

üöÄ Step-by-Step Workflow

1

Start the Visualizer

2

Choose Your Data

Decide whether to use random numbers or enter your own custom numbers.

3

Select Algorithm

Press B, I, S, Q, M, or H to choose your sorting algorithm.

4

Choose Direction

Press A for ascending or D for descending order.

5

Press SPACE to begin the animated sorting process.

6

Experiment

Try different algorithms, press R for new random data, or X for instant sorting.
